About

I'm Bryce Maxheimer. I work in cybersecurity — primarily on the operations and audit side, with a soft spot for tooling. My day-to-day spans SOC workflows, framework alignment (NIST, MITRE ATT&CK), and database management for evidence and case tracking.

What you'll find here

This site is a working notebook, not a polished portfolio. Expect three kinds of content:

  • Blog posts — writeups of investigations, framework deep-dives, and notes on tools I'm building or evaluating.
  • Scripts — PowerShell modules and one-offs I've found useful enough to keep around. Most are SOC-adjacent.
  • References — cheat sheets and lookup pages I keep coming back to.

Stack

This site is hand-written HTML and CSS, with blog posts authored in Markdown and converted to HTML via a small PowerShell build script. It's hosted on Cloudflare Pages and the source lives on GitHub. No frameworks, no JS bundle, no analytics by default.
