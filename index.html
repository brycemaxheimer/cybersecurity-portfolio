About

I'm a cybersecurity professional with 4+ years of hands-on experience across threat hunting, digital forensics, network defense, and cyber infrastructure management. Most of that came through the U.S. Marine Corps as a Cyber Warfare Operator before transitioning to the federal civilian side.

Today I'm a Shift Lead at the Defense Finance and Accounting Service — running detection workflows in Microsoft Sentinel, building automated toolsets, authoring SIEM rules, and performing digital forensics in support of investigations. I hold an active TS/SCI clearance with CI Polygraph.

I write tools because most SOC work is repetitive and the parts that aren't repetitive deserve more attention than the dashboard usually gives them. The SOC Dashboard project on this site is a self-contained lab environment built around that idea — a place to drill on hunting queries against realistic data without burning ingest budget on a production tenant.

Currently pursuing a B.S. in Applied Cybersecurity at SANS Technology Institute (expected 2027-01).
